# GitLab AI Hackathon — Winning Strategy Report

> Source: Librarian research across Devpost, GitLab blog, hackathon patterns

## The AI Paradox — Key to Winning

GitLab's 2025 Global DevSecOps Report:
- **7 hours/week** lost per team member to AI-related inefficiencies
- **60%** use 5+ tools for software development
- **70%** say AI makes compliance MORE challenging
- **85%** agree agentic AI works best in platform engineering approach

**Judges explicitly look for projects that solve the AI Paradox.**

## Grand Prize Winner Traits (from past Devpost hackathons)

1. **Solves a REAL problem** — not theoretical, not just "cool tech"
2. **Actually works** — zero crashes in demo, clear trigger→action
3. **Exceptional UX** — easy install, configure, interact
4. **Clear narrative** — pain → solution → impact
5. **Deep sponsor integration** — not superficial logo-adding
6. **Technical innovation** — multi-agent, creative tool use
